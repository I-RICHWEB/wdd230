Audience

The organisation is mainly for those who are seeking to improve their jobs and businesses. People who are actively seeking to increase their financial standard. Typically those within the age of 25 years to 60 years. Persons will to contribute to the growth and welfare of others in the society.

He has previously owned a laundry business in Ogun state where he came from Upon his arrival, he applied for a sales rep in one of the local supermarket, but the salary is not a enough to sustain himself and his family.
