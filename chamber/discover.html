Discription: Benin city is one of the commerce center in Nigeria. It is mostly populated by the bini tribe. The binis are know for their hospitality. There is a lot of industries springing up in Benin City now, as such openning employment opportunities for many. Also it has good road and a local airport.

Location: Benin city is located at the center of Edo state, with three of the eighteen local governments in Edo state.

Population: Projected Population, 1st January, 2022 -- 1,605,700
