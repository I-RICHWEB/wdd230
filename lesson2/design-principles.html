Three Design Principles

Uyiosa Richmond Izekor

Repetition

Jumia

jumia.com.ng

Jumia as an online marketing site uses a lot of repetitions on their products gallary to show some form of relationships between the products. We can see how the products images declarations have the same features like the cards sizes, hovering effects, and gallary headers uses the fonts and so on.

Rule Of Thirds

Pinterest

pinterest.com

Pinterest had all their pictures display in the rule of thirds format. By centralizing the focal point of the pictures so the users eyes will easily capture the main focus of the image. It easily get users' attention.

Contrast

Invajy

invajy.com

The site uses a very good contrast in the quotes cards. The color contrast between the quotes and the images are very distinct. By either using a bright color on the text that has a dark background image or they blur out the image in the area of the text to create a very powerful contrast.
